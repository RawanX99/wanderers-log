title:
A Morning Above the Clouds

body:
I woke up earlier than planned during my stay in Kyoto and decided to take a short walk before sunrise. The streets were completely empty, and the air felt unusually fresh.

After walking for about 20 minutes, I found a small path leading uphill. I followed it without knowing where it would go. The climb was a bit tiring, but when I reached the top, I was completely surprised.

The entire city was covered in a thin layer of fog, and only the tops of buildings and distant mountains were visible above it. As the sun slowly rose, the light spread across the fog, creating a view that looked almost unreal.

I stayed there for nearly half an hour, just watching everything change. It was one of the quietest and most peaceful moments of my trip.

Definitely not something I planned—but easily one of the best experiences I had.
